Gymnopédie No. 3
================

Composer: Erik Satie
Year: 1888
Status: PUBLIC DOMAIN — free legal download

Key: A minor (with that wandering Satie feel)
Time: 3/4
Tempo: Lent et grave — quarter note ≈ 60 bpm
Difficulty: Advanced beginner. Same accessibility as No. 1, slightly different mood.

Why include it after No. 1
--------------------------
- Same LH technique (bass note + chord pattern)
- Slightly different harmonic colors — more melancholic
- Once you have No. 1's pattern in your hands, this one comes quickly
- Great for building your "Satie vocabulary"

Where to download (free, legal)
-------------------------------
- IMSLP: https://imslp.org/wiki/Trois_Gymnop%C3%A9dies_(Satie%2C_Erik)
  (same "Complete Score" PDF — all 3 Gymnopédies in one file)
- Mutopia Project: https://www.mutopiaproject.org/cgibin/make-table.cgi?Composer=SatieE

Practice tips
-------------
1. Same LH approach as No. 1 — bass note then chord, change pedal with each chord
2. Watch for the chromatic moments in the melody — they're the soul of the piece
3. Pair this with No. 1 for a 5-minute mini-recital
