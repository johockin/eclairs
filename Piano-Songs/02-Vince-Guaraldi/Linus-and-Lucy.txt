Linus and Lucy
==============

Composer: Vince Guaraldi
Year: 1964
Status: COPYRIGHTED — placeholder. Purchase or borrow legally.

Key: Ab major
Time: 4/4
Tempo: Bright, around quarter note ≈ 132 bpm
Difficulty: Tricky for an advanced beginner. The LH ostinato is the iconic
            sound — but it's a stretch and requires hand independence.
            GET THE EASY PIANO ARRANGEMENT, not the transcription.

The challenge
-------------
- The famous LH part is an ostinato in 10ths (Ab to Eb to C and back)
- The "10th" is too wide for many beginner hands — easy arrangements
  reduce it to an octave or break it into a roll
- The RH melody is syncopated against the LH groove — that's the hard part
  for an advanced beginner

Where to buy/download (legal)
-----------------------------
- Musicnotes.com: search "Linus and Lucy Guaraldi"
  - **EASY PIANO** version exists (~$5) — get this one first
  - Piano Solo (transcription) also available — save for later
- SheetMusicPlus.com: same
- Hal Leonard "A Charlie Brown Christmas" songbook has it
- Hal Leonard "Vince Guaraldi Collection" has the more advanced version

Practice tips
-------------
1. Start with the easy version. No shame.
2. Learn the LH alone until it's ABSOLUTELY automatic. You should be able to
   talk while playing it. Then add the RH.
3. Drop the tempo way down — like half speed — until your hands stop fighting.
4. The bridge changes feel completely — let yourself relax into it.
5. Reference recording: Vince Guaraldi Trio's original (1964 Fantasy Records)
