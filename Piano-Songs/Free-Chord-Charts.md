# Free UGC Chord Charts

User-generated chord charts (free) for the non-classical songs in this folder.
For the Satie pieces, get actual sheet music from IMSLP — chord charts don't make sense for classical solo piano.

## Quick reference: which site for which kind of song

| Song type | Best site | Why |
|-----------|-----------|-----|
| Pop / soul (Stevie Wonder) | **Ultimate Guitar** | Massive library, tons of versions per song, "official" tabs are well-curated |
| Jazz standards (Evans, hard bop) | **iRealPro app + LearnJazzStandards.com** | Real lead-sheet-style chord charts in jazz format |
| Anything | **Chordify** | Auto-generated from Spotify/YouTube — quick reference, accuracy varies |

## A note on legality

UGC chord chart sites operate in a gray zone — Ultimate Guitar reached a licensing settlement with the National Music Publishers' Association (NMPA) in 2020 and pays publishers, so chord charts there are now licensed. Chordify generates charts from audio it has rights to stream. e-chords and others vary.

Practically: chord charts are a sketch of a song's harmony, not a reproduction of the published sheet music. They're how every pop/jazz musician in the world learns songs, and they're fine for personal/learning use.

If you end up performing or recording, the licensing math is different — but for sitting at home learning piano, you're good.
